For Windows 10 computers:

This is a way to fix your display being too big or small or screen being too bright or dark. Go to your start menu's search bar and search "Settings" it should display an app with a gear icon, then press enter. You should see "" In the center of your screen if you have done it correctly. Navigate to display menu and using the scroll wheel you can find "Scale" "Brightness" and "Resolution". Select something there and it should do the job.

If your computer is slow and you are getting strange popups then follow this article. Also during scanning your computer will lose all data not saved Go to your start menu's search bar and search "Windows Defender". It should show an icon like this when you have searched it: Now open it and select Virus & threat protection then Scan options. Select Microsoft Defender Antivirus (offline scan), and then select Scan. Then it WILL reboot your computer to a terminal-like interface DO NOT TOUCH ANYTHING DURING SCANNING PROCESS!!! then it will restart to plain windows which is when you can normally login, if the problem still exists then go to a repair specialist and do not attempt to repair your windows computer or laptop without proper knowledge.
